Generative engine optimization sounds like a new dark art. It is mostly hygiene.

## Lead with the answer

Models extract the first clear, self-contained statement that answers the query. Put it
near the top, in plain language.

## Make structure machine-legible

Headings, an FAQ, and a clean Markdown rendering of the page do more than any keyword
trick. Strand serves a `.md` version of every article for exactly this reason.

## Cite your sources

Cited claims are trusted more — by Google's E-E-A-T and by the models. Sources are a
first-class field, not an afterthought.
